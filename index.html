About Me

Hi, I'm Justin

I'm a recent graduate from the University of California, San Diego with a B.S. in Machine Learning and Neural Computation under the Cognitive Science program. During my studies, I developed a well rounded understanding of both computer science and data science fundamentals ranging from algorithm design to data modeling, skills that have fostered my understanding of machine learning.

Besides machine learning, I'm also interested in web development, as I find its a great medium to show off other programming/machine learning skills. I love sharing knowledge with others and find that making things visual and interactable only makes them easier to understand.
