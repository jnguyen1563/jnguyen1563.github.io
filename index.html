About Me

Hi, I'm Justin

I'm a recent graduate from the University of California, San Diego with a B.S. in Machine Learning and Neural Computation under the Cognitive Science program. During my studies, I developed a well rounded understanding of both computer science and data science fundamentals thanks to the excellent professors in the Cognitive Science department.

While I am knowledgeable of many machine learning algorithms, I find neural networks to be the most facinating. Due to the flexibility of neural networks, I have found myself using them as models for many of my personal projects, ranging from natural language processing and computer vision.

My Skills

Given my background in machine learning, Python has become my programming language of choice. While I prefer to use Python in the vast majority of my use cases due to its amazing libraries and speedy prototyping, I won't hestitate to switch to or learn another language should it be more appropriate for the project. If speed is a concern for my program, I can switch to a compiled language - with Java being my most comfortable. Of course for web development I'll use HTML, CSS, and JavaScript (along with Flask on occasion). I have listed the languages in the rough order of my competency with them.

As a programmer, I'll never stop finding ways to improve my skills. Right now I am interested in improving my frontend development skills with React and am planning to learn Django to accompany this. I am an advocate for interactivity and would like to eventually convert my machine learning projects into web applications. For machine learning, I have also recently been interested in GANs and am looking into ways I can incorporate them into my future projects.
